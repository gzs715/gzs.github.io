Zhongshu Gu

Research Staff Member, IBM Research

Bio

Zhongshu Gu is a Research Staff Member in the Security Department of the IBM T.J. Watson Research Center.
He received his Ph.D. from Purdue University (Advisor: Prof. Dongyan Xu) and B.S. from Fudan University, both in Computer Science.
His current research interests are in the areas of systems security and AI security.
